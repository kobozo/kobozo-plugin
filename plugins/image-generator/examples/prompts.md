## Tips for Writing Prompts

### Be Specific
❌ Bad: "a logo"
✅ Good: "a modern logo for eco-friendly brand, featuring leaf symbol, minimalist style"

### Include Context
❌ Bad: "product photo"
✅ Good: "product photo for e-commerce listing, white background, professional lighting"

### Specify Style
❌ Bad: "character design"
✅ Good: "character design in minimalist flat style, friendly appearance, tech company mascot"

### Mention Technical Details
❌ Bad: "hero image"
✅ Good: "hero image 16:9 aspect ratio, suitable for website header, modern aesthetic"

### Reference Mood
❌ Bad: "office scene"
✅ Good: "office scene with warm, welcoming atmosphere, natural lighting, professional yet comfortable"

### Include Colors (if specific needed)
❌ Bad: "button"
✅ Good: "button with blue gradient (#4A90E2 to #357ABD), rounded corners"

## Variation Strategies

### Angle Variations

**Base Prompt**: "product photo of smartphone"

**Variations**:
1. "...from front view, screen fully visible"
2. "...from three-quarter angle, showing depth"
3. "...from side profile, emphasizing thin design"

### Color Variations

**Base Prompt**: "abstract background"

**Variations**:
1. "...with warm tones: amber, orange, red"
2. "...with cool tones: blue, teal, purple"
3. "...with vibrant saturated colors, high energy"

### Style Variations

**Base Prompt**: "character illustration"

**Variations**:
1. "...in photorealistic 3D rendered style"
2. "...in clean vector illustration style"
3. "...in minimalist flat design style"
